Welcome to Lisha Traders. These terms and conditions outline the rules and regulations for the use of our services and website.

1. General Wholesale Supply

Lisha Traders operates as a premium wholesale supplier of Indian green cardamom. All offers, sales, quotations, and supply contracts are subject to confirmation, and any transaction is completed upon direct execution of a formal wholesale agreement between both parties.

2. Cardamom Grading & Specifications

Cardamom grades are supplied according to domestic and export specifications (6.0 mm to 9.0 mm ranges). Product details, sieve limits, moisture tolerance, and Liter weight measurements correspond to specifications outlined by the Spices Board of India.

3. Pricing & Fluctuation

Cardamom market prices fluctuate on a daily basis according to auction indexes at Bodinayakanur and Vandanmedu auction houses. Any prices generated via online estimators are indicative only and do not bind Lisha Traders to sell at specific price ranges until contract execution.
