Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

At Lisha Traders, accessible from our site, one of our main priorities is the privacy of our visitors. This Privacy Policy document contains types of information that is collected and recorded by Lisha Traders and how we use it.

1. Information We Collect

We collect voluntary contact information submitted through our wholesale quote calculators, email forms, and WhatsApp inquiries. This information includes company name, representative name, phone number, email address, and product specification requirements.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected details to prepare formal wholesale price quotations, schedule sample shipments, provide factory tour availability, and respond to bulk pricing inquiries.

3. Direct Communication

Lisha Traders communicates with users exclusively through official direct channels (company phone, corporate email, or verified WhatsApp) to finalize supply contracts and trade agreements.
